WELCOME TO Binpur-II Government ITI

Established in 2016, BINPUR-II GOVERNMENT ITI is approved by the National Skill Council Of Vocational Training. It is a quality training institution that offers in-depth knowledge and professional training in the fields of Electrician, Wireman, Surveyor, Sewing Technology, Mechanic Motor Vehicle, Welder, Fitter, and COPA.

WHY CHOOSE BINPUR-II GOVERMENT ITI ?

With an amazing learning environment and skilled teaching professionals, this institution provides complete knowledge about different industrial sectors so that trainees can be self-independent in the future and contribute to the reinforcement of the industrial strength of the country. This institution not only sharpens the skills of the students but also provides them with suitable job opportunities. WE WILL PROVIDE THE ENVIRONMENT TO CREATE GREAT ENTREPRENEURS AND GLOBALLY EMPLOYABLE ITI GRADUATES.
